ABOUT US

Discover Tranquility and Luxury: Your Private Escape Awaits

Welcome to Tranquil Escape Villa

‍

Discover an unparalleled luxury experience nestled in nature’s embrace. At our villa, we offer the perfect escape from city life, where privacy, tranquility, and indulgence come together for a memorable stay.

Our Vision

‍

We are dedicated to providing our guests with a serene and luxurious retreat, far from the noise of everyday life. Surrounded by lush greenery, our villa is designed to help you unwind and reconnect with nature.

What Sets Us Apart

‍

Our secluded location offers peace and privacy, ideal for honeymooners, families, and nature lovers. Our villa boasts:

  • Luxurious Rooms: Elegant, comfortable, and designed with sophistication.
  • Romantic Terrace: A perfect setting for unforgettable date nights.
  • Exquisite Dining: Experience flavorful, artfully crafted meals that captivate your palate.
  • Private Swimming Pool: Relax and unwind in our luxurious outdoor pool.

Exceptional Services

‍

We go above and beyond to ensure your comfort. Our dedicated staff is always ready to assist with any need, ensuring that your stay is perfect from start to finish. Every detail, from plush bedding to curated amenities, is crafted for ultimate relaxation and luxury. We offer:

  • Airport Transfers
  • Tour packages with professional guides
  • Car and bike rentals

Explore Hikkaduwa

‍

Just 1.5 km from the center of Hikkaduwa, our villa offers easy access to the town's vibrant nightlife, delicious street food, seafood, and a variety of water activities. Whether you're exploring the famous Hikkaduwa beach or enjoying the local culture, there’s always something to discover.
